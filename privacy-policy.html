Privacy Policy

Odeeffannoo Dhuunfaa Ilaalchisee Imaammata Keenya

Guyyaa Haaromfame: Waxabajjii 2025

Baha Biiftuu ("nuti", "keenya") imaammata privacy kana kan qopheessine namoota website fi social media channels keenya daawwatan, content keenya fayyadaman, ykn tajaajila keenya fayyadaman irraa odeeffannoo akkamitti argannuu fi fayyadamnu ibsuuf dha.

1. Odeeffannoo Maal Arganna?

Yeroo channels fi website keenya daawwattan, odeeffannoo armaan gadii argachuu dandeenya:

  • Maqaa fi email address — yoo nuuf ergittan qofaa
  • Lakkoofsa daawwannaa fi engagement (YouTube Analytics, Facebook Insights)
  • Browser fi device odeeffannoo (cookies fi analytics tools dhaan)
  • Subscription ykn follow irraa waliigaltee

2. Odeeffannoo Kana Maaliif Fayyadamna?

  • Content haaraa fi gaarii uumuuf
  • Channels keenya fooyyessuuf
  • Gaaffii fi yaada keessaniif deebii kennuuf
  • Tajaajilawwan keenya isinitti beeksiisuuf

3. Odeeffannoo Eenyuuf Kennina?

Odeeffannoo keessan dhaabbilee sadaffaa irraa HIN gurgurru fi HIN kenninu. Haa ta'u malee, seeraan dirqisiifamne yoo ta'e ykn YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Telegram fi X (Twitter) irraa analytics data walitti qabna — kanneen policy mataa isaanii qabu.

4. Platforms Sadaffaa

Content keenya platforms armaan gadii irratti argachuu dandeessu:

  • YouTube — Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook / Instagram — Meta Privacy Policy
  • Telegram — Telegram Privacy Policy
  • X (Twitter) — X Privacy Policy

5. Cookies

Website keenya analytics fi experience fooyyessuuf cookies fayyadama. Browser keessan irraa cookies dhaabuu dandeessu — garuu tajaajila tokko tokko sirriitti hojjechuu dhiisuu danda'a.

6. Daa'imman Ilaalchisee

Content keenya umuriin 13 gadi ta'aniif kan qophaa'e miti. Daa'imni odeeffannoo isaa kennite yoo beekne, nuu quunnamaa — ni haafna.

7. Mirga Keessan

  • Odeeffannoo keessan maal akka ta'e gaafachuu dandeessu
  • Odeeffannoo sirreessuuf gaafachuu dandeessu
  • Subscription ykn follow haqachuu dandeessu yeroo barbaaddanitti

8. Jijjiirama Imaammata Kanaaf

Imaammata kana yeroo barbaachisaatti haaromsuu dandeenya. Jijjiiramni guddaan yoo jiraate, channels keenya irratti beeksifna. Guyyaan haaromfame gara olii ilaali.
